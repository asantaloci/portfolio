About

Hello, I'm Angel Santaloci, a UX/UI designer and front-end developer.

I'm an undergraduate senior at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill studying Computer Science and Information Science, with a specialization in Human Computer Interaction design. I'm passionate about the possibilities in merging my passions of art, design, and technology together.

I strive to create thoughtful, detailed design solutions that balance client, business, and user needs. I can communicate and collaborate with both tech and business to elevate their products and experiences.

Right now, I am an UX/Tech Intern at Cisco where I make user experience solutions for the internal workflow of their current distributor systems, and redesign and code some of their HR tools. Previously, I was the Director of Design for Pearl Hacks, a women and non-binary hackathon in UNC-CH for two years, where I took my design knowledge and incorporated my skills into developing an inclusive brand for years to come. On the side, I do commissions for UX work and other design jobs.

In my freetime, I enjoy making traditional and digital art, consuming salmon nigiri, and playing music with my unofficial band.
