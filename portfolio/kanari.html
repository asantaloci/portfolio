INTRO An effective, multi-faceted platform designed for music collaboration for the startup Kanari.

Duration: 1 week

Year: 2019

Role: UX Designer


Objective

I was tasked to create an innovative platform to foster effective music collaboration.

Summary

This is a user-researched, fully-developed app design for the challenge of making an effective music collaboration app for Kanari, a startup for music creatives. I interviewed likely users, studied and created user stories, developed an ideation process with detailed summaries of the elements of my final design, and created a high-fidelity wireframe to showcase the functionality of my work in a week.

Research

The process of creating this interface including the development of general and specific user stories, ideation process, design choices, and the complete explanation of the interface will be found here.

Lo-Fi Review

I liked the main menu area that I designed, however the navigation bar was lacking in style and usability. Thus, I implemented a square-shaped navigation tool to allow the user to explore the navigation tools such as projects, clients, and teams, as well as keep the exploration contained in an organized section. Plus, I just thought the interface looked cleaner and more aesthetic this way.
